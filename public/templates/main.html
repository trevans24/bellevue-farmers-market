Bellevue Farmers Market

Experience the Market

BFM operates a seasonal Thursday farmers market, held every week rain or shine from May - October. Each market day is composed of its own unique mix of vendors offering seasonal fruits & vegetables, cut flowers, fresh meats, one-of-a-kind artisan goods, and tasty cuisine. All BFM vendors grow and make the products they sell, and we are proud to say everything sold at our market comes from within the great state of Washington.

Whether you are looking for fresh ingredients, a grab-and-go meal, or a gift for someone special – we’ve got you covered.
