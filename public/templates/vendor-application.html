BECOME A VENDOR

PLEASE NOTE THE BELLEVUE FARMERS MARKET ACCEPTS ONLY FOOD AND FLOWER FARMERS AND BUSINESSES WITHIN WASHINGTON STATE BORDERS TO SELL AT THE MARKET. RESELLING OF ANY TYPE IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED.

For those that meet this criteria, please read the 2017 Market Rules and Regulations BEFORE APPLYING.

APPLY TO THE MARKET

The Bellevue Farmers Market uses an online registration website called Manage My Market. You must complete two steps on Manage My Market to apply:

1. CREATE A PROFILE FOR YOUR BUSINESS

  • Complete a profile for your business on Manage My Market.
  • If you’re applying for the first time, click register now to create an account on Manage My Market.
  • You will select “I’m a Vendor” after clicking “register now”.

    • PLEASE NOTE:

    When completing your profile, please provide a valid email address. All future communications will be made via the email address provided. When you arrive at the “My Products” page of your profile, please list every product that you would like to sell at the Bellevue Farmers Market. The more specific you are, the more it will benefit your 2017 application.

    2. COMPLETE AND SUBMIT THE MARKET APPLICATION

  • After completing and/or updating your business profile, you’re ready to begin filling out the 2017 Application.
  • Select “Click Here to Apply to a Market” and use the zip code 98004 to find the Bellevue Farmers Market.
  • Follow the simple directions while filling out each page of the application. Remember to click SAVE at the bottom of each page!

    • PLEASE NOTE:

    Select only the Thursdays you will attend the market. Do not click “select all” if you do not intend to apply for all Thursdays.
